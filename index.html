Ayoung Chun

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Program on American Institutional Renewal (PAIR) at Purdue University. I received my Ph.D. in Political Science from UCLA in 2025.

I study campaign finance, legislative institutions, and political representation in the United States. My research examines how relationships between donors and elected officials develop, how political institutions structure those relationships, and how financial support shapes legislative behavior in Congress and state legislatures.

My work has been published in Legislative Studies Quarterly and has received awards from the APSA State Politics and Policy Section and the APSA Political Organizations and Parties Section. I am on the 2026–2027 academic job market.
